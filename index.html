What is village Banking ?

Village banking is a way for people who know and trust each other(like friends,coworkers , or neighbors) to save and borrow money together , without needing a bank. A typical group has 5 to 10 members.They set their own rules, elect leaders , and meet once in a week normally a friday to give out loans , track repayments and track there savings.
